About Playhouse SMP

We're Playhouse SMP, a vanilla survival Java server for adults (18+). Looking for a friendly, steady, and tight community? This is it!

Playhouse SMP has been around for five years. The main reason we've made it this far is that we put a lot of effort and care into our community.

We're currently on Season 7 with a brand new world in 1.21.8!

Between resets, we often have community events and side worlds like Skyblock, Create, Cobblemon, Hardcore, and more.

Our server runs on a dedicated host in the US, providing excellent performance with 99%+ uptime.

Our Journey

June 2020 - The Beginning

Started by friends from different servers looking for a steady place to play at their own pace

2020-2022 - Seasons 1-4

Community grew to 50+ players worldwide with events like Backwoods hardcore mode

2023 - Season 5

Trails & Tales update with amplified worlds and escape mansion events

2024 - Season 6

Tricky Trials update - Extra long season with Skyblock, Create Mod, and Cobblemon events

2025 - Season 7

Currently thriving! New world with custom items, community projects, and more adventures ahead

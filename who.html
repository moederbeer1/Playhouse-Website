Who Are We?

Playhouse is a Minecraft server for adults. We play survival and the difficulty is set to hard. Our server runs on PaperMC and is on a dedicated host, providing excellent performance and stability.

History: Our server was started in June 2020 by a group of friends from different servers who wanted a steady place to play at their own pace.

Community: We build communal farms and collaborate on big projects like the spawn, shopping district, and nether hub. It's a group effort where everyone can contribute.

Playerbase: We have about 50 active players from around the world, ensuring there's always someone online. Most of our players are between 25 and 50 years old.
